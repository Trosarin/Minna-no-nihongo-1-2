Tip for distinguishing

🔍 そう [様態] has the word shortening (高そう, 降りそう) — other forms leave the word intact.

🔍 らしい = you have it heard, そう [伝聞] = you know it concretely (e.g., from news).

🔍 ようだ = you see indications, みたい = like ようだ but more casual.

Negation forms

formmeaning

formExamplemeaning
